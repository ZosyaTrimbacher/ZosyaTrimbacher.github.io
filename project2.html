Overview

Goal: Identify priority markets, revenue-driving genres, and high-value customer segments to guide Rockbuster’s global streaming rollout.

Role: Data Analyst

Timeline: 2 weeks

Tools: SQL, Tableau

Process

Research

  • Explored revenue distribution across countries, cities, and customer tiers.
  • Benchmarked genre performance to inform catalog prioritization.
  • Reviewed historical rental patterns to highlight retention opportunities.

Design / Implementation

  • Queried the transactional database; joined payments, rentals, films, categories, customers, and locations with SQL.
  • Applied CTEs, subqueries, and window functions to rank markets, titles, and customers by revenue.
  • Built Tableau visuals for genre revenue, top markets, and customer value tiers.

Testing / Results

  • Genres: Sports and Sci-Fi generated the highest revenue; Animation, Drama, and Comedy followed.
  • Markets: Revenue concentrated in a handful of high-value countries — ideal for phased rollout.
  • Customers: High-value tiers rented more frequently and responded to targeted incentives.

Outcome

Delivered a prioritized market entry plan, genre focus for catalog acquisition, and loyalty tactics to maximize early streaming ROI.
