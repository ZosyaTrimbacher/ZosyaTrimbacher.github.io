Overview

Goal: Estimate staffing needs during U.S. influenza season and communicate a clear plan via a Tableau storyboard.

Role: Data Analyst • Tools: Tableau, Excel • Duration: ~2 weeks

What I did

  • Cleaned and merged CDC influenza deaths with U.S. Census demographics.
  • Exploratory analysis by age group; one-tailed t-test to confirm elderly risk.
  • Tiered states by vulnerability; built spatial/temporal visuals in Tableau.

Outcome

Actionable seasonal staffing windows (Dec–Feb peak) and high-need state tiers to guide allocation.
