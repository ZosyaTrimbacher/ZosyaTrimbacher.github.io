Overview

Goal: Analyze influenza season trends across the United States to identify hospital staffing needs.
Role: Data Analyst
Timeline: 2 weeks
Tools: Tableau, Excel

Process

Research

  • Collected U.S. influenza case data by region and week.
  • Explored seasonality patterns in hospital admissions.
  • Benchmarked staffing capacity against peak flu season demand.

Design / Implementation

  • Cleaned and structured raw data in Excel.
  • Built interactive Tableau dashboards and a storyboard for stakeholders.
  • Created choropleth maps and weekly trend visualizations.

Testing / Results

  • Identified staffing shortages in key states during peak weeks.
  • Validated storyboard usability with peer feedback.

Outcome

The storyboard provides hospital administrators with actionable insights to adjust staffing levels during flu season. Future improvements include integrating real-time CDC data and expanding dashboards to cover multiple years.
